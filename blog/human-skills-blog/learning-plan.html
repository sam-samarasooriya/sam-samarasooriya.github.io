I believe that learning style is unique to every person. if I consider the standard learning methods, I mostly use the Visual learning style as I am good at retaining information when it is presented in many visual ways. However, my deep passion for becoming a developer pushes me to keep working on this boot camp. I believe that passion and pushing myself to stay focused are my strengths. Also, I'm using my analytical skills and problem-solving skills to become successful in this boot camp.
