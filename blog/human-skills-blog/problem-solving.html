What is Problem Solving

Problem solving is the process of identifying a challenge or obstacle and finding a solution to overcome it. It involves several steps:

  • Defining the problem,
  • Defining the problem
  • Analyzing the situation
  • Brainstorming potential solutions
  • evaluating options
  • implementing the best solution
